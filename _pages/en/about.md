<div class="bio-text">
  <strong>Prof. Yinliang (Ricky) Tan</strong> is a Professor of Decision Sciences and Management Information Systems at <strong>CEIBS (China Europe International Business School)</strong>. He was a visiting scholar at the <strong>Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence and Digital Economy Lab</strong>. Prior to joining CEIBS, he was a tenured Professor, Bauer Endowed Chair Professor, DBA Program Director, and Area Chair of Supply Chain Management at the <strong>C.T. Bauer College of Business, University of Houston</strong>. He also served as Associate Director of the AI Research Center and Senior Fellow of the HPE Data Science Institute at the University of Houston. Before that, he held positions of Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, and Administrative Director of the Goldring Institute for International Education at <strong>Tulane University's A.B. Freeman School of Business</strong>, where he earned tenure and an endowed chair.
</div>

<div class="bio-text">
  <strong>Prof. Tan</strong> received his Ph.D. from the <strong>Warrington College of Business, University of Florida</strong>, specializing in Operations Management and Information Systems. His research focuses on <strong>Artificial Intelligence</strong> and the <strong>Digital Economy</strong>, drawing on extensive industry experience and collaborative practice with firms in both China and the United States. He is also an accomplished educator in data analytics and was honored with the <strong>Freeman School of Business Teacher of the Year Award</strong>.
</div>

<div class="bio-text">
  <strong>Prof. Tan</strong> has published over <strong>20</strong> papers in the top 50 international management journals recognized by the <em>Financial Times</em>, including <em>Management Science</em>, <em>MIS Quarterly</em>, <em>Information Systems Research</em>, <em>Production and Operations Management</em>, <em>Manufacturing & Service Operations Management</em>, and <em>Human Relations</em>, receiving multiple best paper awards.
</div>

<div class="bio-text">
  <strong>Prof. Tan</strong> currently serves as <strong>Senior Editor</strong> of <em>Production and Operations Management</em>, <strong>Department Editor</strong> of <em>Decision Sciences Journal</em>, <strong>Associate Editor</strong> of <em>Information & Management</em>, <em>Information Systems Research</em>, and <em>Decision Support Systems</em>, and as an editorial board member of <em>Journal of Operations Management</em> and <em>Journal of Management Information Systems</em>. He also serves on the jury of the <strong>Harvard Business Review</strong> "Dinggee Award" and as a member of the Standardization Committee of the <strong>Shanghai AI Technology Association</strong>.
</div>

<div class="bio-text">
  <strong>Prof. Tan</strong> was named one of the <strong>World's Best 40 Under 40 Business School Professors</strong> in 2019. That same year, he received the <strong>Decision Sciences Institute Early Career Achievement Award</strong>. In 2022, he was awarded the <strong>INFORMS ISS Sandy Slaughter Early Career Award</strong> for his contributions to the information systems field. In 2025, he received the <strong>CEIBS Research Excellence Award</strong> and the <strong>European Case Clearing House (ECCH) Best Case Award in the AI Category</strong>.
</div>
